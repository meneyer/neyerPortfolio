ABOUT ME

BACKGROUND

I have been working in the audio/visual sector of the live events industry for over 13 years, an industry that has drastically changed due to the COVID-19 pandemic. I began as an entry level employee with very little technical knowledge. Through hard work, dedication, and a willingness to learn and listen, I have been through several promotions and currently am employed as a Project Manager.

I work hard, possess the ability to learn and adapt, and keep a positive outlook and attitude. I am organized, detail-oriented, and have the ability to get along with most anyone. These skills have led to many other opportunities inside the company to develop new systems and offer insight to improve current processes. I enjoy assisting in improving company culture and served on a committee to create and implement company ideals.

EXPERIENCES IN LIVE EVENTS

The live events industry is a fast-paced environment consisting of tight deadlines and ever-changing circumstances. It has taught me important skills such as troubleshooting, how to stay calm under immense pressure, and developing the ability to work with a wide variety of personnel including (but not limited to) clients, co-workers, freelance workers, union staff, and venue employees. I have grown both personally and professionally as I traveled around the country working on events. The biggest compliment I have ever received is knowing that a client is happy to see me present for their specific event. It is truly as honor to know that someone has put their trust in my abilities and it is my goal to make each client feel that way from the beginning of the planning process through the conclusion of their event.

Due to my commitment to customer service, I was nominated for a Rose Award in 2013. The ROSE (Recognition of Service Excellence) Award is an industry award given to employees that excel in customer service, workplace attitude, and go above and beyond expectations. Nominations are made by the nominee's employer and letters of recommendation are received from customers explaining why the nominee is deserving of the award. It was truly an honor to be recognized and nominated.
