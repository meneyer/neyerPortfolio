ABOUT ME

BACKGROUND

I have been working in the audio/visual sector of the live events industry for over 13 years. I began as an entry level employee with very little technical knowledge and have worked my way up the ladder through several different positions to my current role as a Project Manager.

I have always worked hard in my career, been a continual learner, and have kept a positive attitude along the way. I am a very organized and detail-oriented person and someone that gets along well with people. This has led to many other opportunities inside the company to develop new systems and offer insight to improve current processes. I enjoy assisting in improving company culture and have served on committees to create and implement company ideals.



EXPERIENCES IN LIVE EVENTS

The live-events industry is a fast-paced industry with tight deadlines and ever-changing circumstances and timelines. It has taught me a lot about troubleshooting, how to work with a wide variety of people (clients, freelancers, union, co-workers), and how to stay calm under immense pressure. I have grown a lot as a person as I have traveled all around the country working on events. The biggest compliment I ever received from a client was when she told me her blood pressure lowered when she saw me on her event. I am honored that I was able to gain her complete trust and strive to do that for every client.

As an audio/visual technician, I have been constantly challenged to keep learning. Technicians must stay up to date with the latest technologies to be able to put complex audio and video systems together. We must pay attention to even the smallest of details as one bad pin on a cable can cause hours of troubleshooting. As a continual learner, I am now focused on website development as the live events industry has changed due to the pandemic. Instead of gathering large groups of people together in small spaces, the industry has transitioned to virtual events. Website development has become a critical part of the industry's new normal and a skill I am excited to continue to grow in.
